Support

We offer a handful of support channels for making sure your questions find answers.

Issues

Github Issues

Since this project is all publicly available on GitHub we use the issues for the home repo for general support.

Direct

Email

If your support needs are more private you are welcome to just email us any questions or concerns directly.

Social

Twitter

We like to hear from you on Twitter, helping make your questions accessible to the entire community via social media.

Contribute

Pull Request

If you see something that needs fixed or done to any of the Union Fasion projecs you are welcome to make a pull request.
